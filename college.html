Welcomeee to Model Polytechnic College Karunagappally

Your Trusted Partner in Innovation

WModel Polytechnic College, Karunagappally is running under the Institute of Human Resource Development established by Government of Kerala. The college offers three year Engineering programmes in Diploma level like Electrical and Electronics Engineering,Electronics and Communication Engineering,Computer Hardware Engineering,Computer Engineering and Electronics Engineering.These courses are affiliated to the Directorate of Technical Education, Kerala and approved by All India Council For Technical Education (AICTE). All the courses have been tailored to suit the extreme industry requirements. The college aims at making the students technically competent and socially committed with a strong base of sound professional ethics so as to make them better citizens of tomorrow. Model Polytechnic College, Karunagappally moulds the students in such a way as to launch them confidently into the 21st century, an era of information technology. The institution is dedicated to scientific advancement, technological progress and economic growth of the country through human resource development.

— Mission & Vision —

INSTITUTION VISION

Strive to develop an institution of excellence in technical education to provide affordable quality education to produce skilled and trained engineers with competence, integrity and social commitment and thus to inspire, empower our students to be lifelong learners, innovative thinkers, ethical leaders and entrepreneurs

INSTITUTION MISSION

• Our mission is to provide quality technical education and training to prepare students for successful careers in a dynamic and evolving job market
• We strive to create a stimulating learning environment that fosters academic excellence, innovation, and hands-on practical experience.
• We encourage creativity and entrepreneurship by providing students with the necessary resources and mentorship to develop and commercialize their ideas.
