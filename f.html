Overview

The Computer Engineering Department offers a comprehensive curriculum that blends electrical engineering and computer science principles to equip students with the skills needed for designing, developing, and optimizing computer systems and networks. The department covers a wide range of topics, including computer architecture, embedded systems, software engineering, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, data structures, and algorithms. Students gain hands-on experience in both hardware and software, preparing them for careers in industries such as tech, telecommunications, robotics, and cybersecurity, as well as for further research and development in cutting-edge technologies.

Our Mission

The goal is to provide a cutting-edge education in computer engineering that equips students with both theoretical and practical knowledge. This includes hands-on experience and critical thinking skills, enabling them to design and implement complex systems. The focus is on fostering creativity, collaboration, and ethical responsibility, preparing graduates to be innovative problem-solvers and leaders who positively impact society and advance the field.

Our Vision

We aim to cultivate a diverse and inclusive community of students, faculty and staff who share a passion for technology and a commitment to excellence. We aim to provide a supportive and stimulating leaming environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and lifelong leaming.
