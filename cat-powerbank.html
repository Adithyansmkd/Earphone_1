Features of Sonic

Sonic PowerBank offers fast charging with dual USB ports, allowing you to charge multiple devices simultaneously. Its compact, lightweight design makes it highly portable, perfect for travel. Sonic Wireless

PowerBank’s wireless charging feature lets you power up your devices without cables. Simply place your smartphone on the charging pad for a quick, hassle-free charge. It supports Qi-enabled devices and delivers efficient wireless power. With a sleek design, it’s ideal for travel or daily use, offering both wired and wireless charging for maximum convenience and flexibility on the go.
