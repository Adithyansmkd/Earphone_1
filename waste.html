About EcoTrack Solutions

Pioneering Sustainable Waste Management

At EcoTrack Solutions, we're revolutionizing how the world manages waste. Founded in 2018, our mission is to transform environmental challenges into opportunities for sustainable growth and innovation.

Our cutting-edge technology allows for real-time tracking and optimization of waste collection, processing, and recycling. By leveraging AI and IoT solutions, we've helped municipalities reduce operational costs by up to 30% while significantly decreasing their carbon footprint.

We believe in a future where waste management is not just about disposal, but about creating a circular economy where resources are preserved and reused efficiently. Join us in our journey toward a cleaner, more sustainable planet.

Our Services

Waste Disposal

We have developed strong relationships with disposal facilities, landfills and incinerators. These relationships utilize only fully permitted and licensed facilities to safely manage your waste.

Waste Treatment

We have used biological, chemical, or mechanical methods to remove pollutants from industrial or municipal wastes, change the character and composition of medical waste or reduce its potential for harm.

Waste Recycling

Recycling is processing common waste materials into new, useful products. It reduces energy consumption, reduces pollution of water and landfills; reducing the need for waste disposal.

Waste Collection & Transportation

We utilize a network of qualified, licensed and insured Transportation companies. We thoroughly audit each transporter to ensure they have the proper insurance and permit to transport your waste safely.
