Welcome to Model Polytechnic College Karunagappally

Your Trusted Partner in Innovation

Model Polytechnic College, Karunagappally is running under the Institute of Human Resource Development, established by the Government of Kerala. The college offers three-year Engineering programs at the Diploma level in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Computer Hardware Engineering, Computer Engineering, and Electronics Engineering. These courses are affiliated with the Directorate of Technical Education, Kerala, and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). All the programs are designed to meet the dynamic needs of the industry. We are proud to announce our partnership with Spoken Tutorial, IIT Bombay, MoE, Govt. of India as our Knowledge Partner for the academic year 2024-2025. This collaboration will provide our students with enhanced learning opportunities and access to cutting-edge resources, furthering their skills and expertise in various technical fields. Our aim is to make our students technically competent and socially committed, instilling a strong base of professional ethics to help them become better citizens of tomorrow. Model Polytechnic College, Karunagappally shapes students to confidently enter the 21st century, an era dominated by information technology. We are committed to contributing to scientific advancement, technological progress, and the economic growth of the country through human resource development.

— Mission & Vision —

INSTITUTION VISION

Strive to develop an institution of excellence in technical education to provide affordable quality education to produce skilled and trained engineers with competence, integrity and social commitment and thus to inspire, empower our students to be lifelong learners, innovative thinkers, ethical leaders and entrepreneurs

INSTITUTION MISSION

Our mission is to provide quality technical education and training to prepare students for successful careers in a dynamic and evolving job market
We strive to create a stimulating learning environment that fosters academic excellence, innovation, and hands-on practical experience.
We encourage creativity and entrepreneurship by providing students with the necessary resources and mentorship to develop and commercialize their ideas.
